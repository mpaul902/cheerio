Cheerio

The Motivational Daily Planner

Try it out

What is Cheerio?

The purpose of Cheerio is to aid in college students' quality of life by keeping track of their assignments, while also providing a fun incentive. As students complete tasks throughout the day, they can check them off the to-do list and feed a cheerio to their virtual dog. 

Inspiration

Getting all your work done during a busy week can be really difficult! It can be really easy to procrastinate, or even just forget about a certain assignment until it's too late. This happens to the best of us, and the simple solution seems to be simply writing down your tasks somewhere to remember them. This may seem obvious, yet many students haven't cultivated these organizational habits or just haven't found a suitable medium to do as such.

Although there are already numerous apps that help keep track of events and assignments, not a lot seem to provide any motivation to do so. Cheerio tries to add a little bit of fun to students' mundane daily tasks by giving them a virtual pet dog that they can care for by simply keeping track of and finishing their work. We hope that this added element will give students some more motivation to stay organized and complete their tasks on time.

Functionallity

  • The user types in their task into the input field, and it gets added to their list
  • When a task is completed, they can click the checkbox and watch their virtual pet get fed a cheerio as a reward
  • There is a progress bar each day which fills up gradually as the user completes their tasks
  • There is also a total counter of how many task the user has completed, or more importantly, how many cheerios were fed to their pet
  • They can also tap/click on thier dog to pet them.

Sync Between Your Devices

This app is available on both iOS and macOS, and can seamlessly sync the user's tasks between devices. The macOS app also has a widget that is always accessible at the top of the screen.

Development Process

How it was Made

We used SwiftUI for the user interface layout and functionality. Adobe Illustrator was used for any 2D vector graphics while Adobe After Effects was used to create the pet animations. We used GitHub version control to help recover and store important data.

Challenges

We had a few issues with the database we were using to store user data as it did not seem to work with macOS too well. We were almost ready to scrap the cross-device syncing idea, but we managed to find a different database manager that wasn't too difficult to learn. We also wanted to create a web app in conjunction with what we had, but the time constraint caused us to pass on that idea.

What We Learned

Creating Cheerio was a great experience where we learned how to to create and convert a macOS app to iOS. It also taught us how to better design and implement ideas while properly managing our own time when working on such a project with limited time and personal.

Plans for the Future

We hope to add some of the functionallity we couldn't get to, such as displaying an insperational quote every day. We would also like to increase the variety of pets to choose from so each user can find the animal that speaks to them the most to hopefully motivate them to stay organized and complete more tasks each day.
